Procedure Overview

What is Liposuction?

Liposuction is a body contouring procedure designed to remove stubborn fat deposits that are resistant to diet and exercise. It is not a weight-loss solution but rather a precision sculpting tool for patients who are close to their ideal body weight and struggling with localised areas of excess fat.

Common treatment areas include the abdomen, flanks (love handles), thighs, inner knees, upper arms, back, and the submental area (chin). Multiple regions can often be addressed in a single session, making liposuction an efficient and comprehensive contouring option.

Dr. Matwa Christopher employs the tumescent liposuction technique - a saline solution containing a local anaesthetic and adrenaline is infused into the target tissue before fat removal. This approach significantly reduces bleeding, bruising, and post-operative discomfort, shortening recovery time and improving safety compared to traditional dry liposuction methods.

Benefits of Precision Liposuction

◎

Targeted Fat Removal

Liposuction addresses specific, localised fat deposits that diet and exercise cannot resolve - delivering precisely the contour improvement you are looking for.

◈

Improved Body Contours

By removing excess fat from strategic areas, the procedure reveals the underlying muscular definition and creates a smoother, more sculpted silhouette.

✦

Minimal Scarring

Liposuction uses tiny incisions (3-5mm) through which the cannula is inserted. These heal to small, barely visible marks that fade further over time.

◉

Long-Lasting Results

Fat cells removed by liposuction do not regenerate. As long as a healthy lifestyle is maintained, results are permanent - making liposuction a durable investment.

✧

Multiple Areas in One Session

Several treatment zones - for example, abdomen, flanks, and thighs - can be addressed in a single procedure, reducing total downtime and anaesthesia exposure.

⊕

Fast Recovery

Tumescent technique means less bleeding and bruising. Most patients return to light daily activities within a week and feel substantially recovered within 2-3 weeks.

How It Works

The Procedure

Dr. Matwa Christopher follows a systematic, safety-first approach to liposuction - ensuring precise fat removal with optimal aesthetic outcomes and minimal recovery time.

1

Consultation & Area Marking

A detailed pre-operative consultation assesses your target areas, skin quality, and fat distribution. Treatment zones are clearly marked on standing skin to guide precise fat removal and ensure symmetrical results.

2

Tumescent Infusion

A tumescent solution - saline combined with a local anaesthetic (lidocaine) and a vasoconstrictive agent (adrenaline) - is infused into the treatment area. This numbs the tissue, constricts blood vessels to minimise blood loss, and causes the fat to swell for easier removal.

3

Precision Cannula Fat Removal

Small incisions (3-5mm) are made and a thin hollow tube (cannula) is inserted. The cannula is moved in controlled, fanning motions to dislodge and suction out fat cells. Dr. Matwa works in multiple planes to ensure smooth, even contours without surface irregularities.

4

Compression & Recovery

Incisions are closed and a medical-grade compression garment is fitted immediately post-procedure. You receive detailed recovery instructions, medications, and a follow-up schedule before discharge. The garment supports healing and helps the skin conform to its new contour.

What to Expect

Your Recovery Timeline

1
Days 1-3
Compression & Rest

Wear the compression garment continuously, 24 hours a day. Expect some swelling, bruising, and fluid drainage from small incision sites - this is normal. Rest at home. Pain is well managed with prescribed medications.

2
Weeks 1-2
Light Activity

Light walking is encouraged to reduce swelling and promote circulation. Avoid heat exposure (saunas, sun, hot baths), strenuous exercise, and lifting. Most swelling and bruising begins to resolve noticeably by the end of this period.

3
Weeks 2-4
Return to Work

Most patients with desk-based or light work return to their professional routine during this phase. Continue wearing the compression garment as directed. Avoid high-intensity exercise for the full 4-week period.

4
Months 2-3
Final Results

Residual swelling continues to resolve and the final contouring results become clearly visible. Skin adapts and tightens over the treated areas. Exercise can be resumed fully. Results are now permanent provided weight is maintained.

Using SPF on the scars and following post-operative care instructions supports the best possible scar outcome.

Following the compression schedule faithfully directly impacts the quality of your final result.
