Our Story

Built on Excellence
and Reliability

Truffel Healthcare was founded with a clear purpose: to make high-quality surgical care accessible to patients in Nairobi and beyond. Located in the heart of Kilimani, we have grown into one of the city's most trusted specialist surgical centres.

We offer a comprehensive range of surgical services — from plastic and aesthetic procedures to reconstructive and general surgery — all under one roof. Our team brings together specialists across multiple disciplines, working in close collaboration to deliver outcomes that matter.

Whether you are exploring cosmetic surgery for the first time or require complex reconstructive care, we are committed to being your partner throughout — before, during, and long after your procedure.

Meet the Surgeon

Expertise You Can
Trust

Dr. Matwa Christopher is a Nairobi-based plastic and reconstructive surgeon and the founder of Truffel Healthcare Kilimani. His career spans over fifteen years of surgical practice, academic teaching, and pioneering research in wound care.

Holding degrees in physiology, medicine, and plastic surgery, Dr. Matwa is fully licensed by the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council (KMPDC). He is a faculty member at Kenya Methodist University and serves as a partner surgeon with Smile Train, performing life-changing cleft lip and palate corrections for children across the region.

His approach combines surgical precision with genuine care — he believes the best outcomes happen when patients are fully informed, respected, and supported at every stage of their journey.

What We Do

Our Areas of
Expertise

Our specialist team covers a broad range of surgical disciplines — ensuring that whatever you need, you receive care from someone who has dedicated their career to it.

✦

Plastic & Aesthetic Surgery

Breast augmentation, reduction, mastopexy, liposuction, tummy tuck, BBL, and lipoabdominoplasty — procedures designed to enhance form and restore confidence.

✦

Reconstructive Surgery

Cleft lip and palate repair, burn reconstruction, scar revision, wound care, and breast reconstruction — restoring function and appearance after injury or illness.

✦

General & Sub-Specialty Surgery

A multidisciplinary team covering otolaryngology, urology, orthopaedics, neurosurgery, paediatric surgery, and cardiothoracic surgery.

✦

Day-Case Surgical Services

Procedures performed and completed within a single day, with full pre-operative preparation and post-operative support — minimal disruption to your life.

✦

Wound Care & Research

Advanced wound management informed by active clinical research, ensuring patients benefit from the most current evidence-based approaches to healing.

✦

Comprehensive After-Care

Recovery does not end in the theatre. We remain your partners through every follow-up, ensuring your outcome matches your expectations.
